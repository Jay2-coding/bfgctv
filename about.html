About Bible Foundation Gospel Church (BFGC)

...The church of heaven bound...

Our Mission

At Bible Foundation Gospel Church, our mission is to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture spiritual growth, and build a loving community that reflects God's love and grace. We are committed to empowering believers to live purposeful lives rooted in faith and service.

Our History

Bible Foundation Gospel Church was Founded in 1998 by Late Pastor Stephen Olabisi Olusina, who died in an accident with his son on the 4th day of June, 2008. BFGC has grown from a small fellowship to a vibrant church family serving the Kano community and beyond. Through faithful teaching, worship, and outreach, we have impacted countless lives and continue to be a beacon of hope and faith.

MISSION STATEMENT

  • Faith: Trusting in God’s promises and living by His Word.
  • Community: Building strong relationships through love and fellowship.
  • Service: Serving God and others with humility and compassion.
  • Growth: Encouraging spiritual maturity and personal development.
  • Outreach: Sharing the gospel and meeting the needs of our neighbors.
